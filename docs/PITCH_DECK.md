## Slide 2 — Problem

Health professionals and creators drown in contradictory literature.

- **250M+ papers** — no human can search them manually
- **Single-source tools** (PubMed-only, writing assistants) miss cross-database agreement
- **Trust gap** — black-box AI summaries without corroboration are unusable in clinical or regulatory contexts
- **Product research pain** — supplement labels, trial IPD, and FDA data live in silos

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

**LENA: ask one question, get a validated evidence brief.**

1. **Parallel search** across 11 databases (PubMed, Cochrane, OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, Europe PMC, trials, WHO, CDC, DailyMed, DSLD, openFDA)
2. **PULSE engine** cross-validates claims across independent sources — transparent confidence scores
3. **Persona-aware synthesis** — clinician, researcher, patient, pharmacist tones
4. **Share & export** — PDF, copy full brief, URL with context (not just a link)
5. **URL/label ingest** — paste a product page or supplement label for grounded search

## Slide 5 — PULSE differentiation
